Project:: STIC

A map of mankind's science history

Wait, What's STIC?

STIC stands for Science Tree for Innovation and Creativity.

This aims to map almost every science histories from ancient to modern science.

Aimed on motivation

Every historic discoveries is connected based on 'motivation' and 'curiosity', like 'what happens if i do like this?'

Easy to view

Thanks to powerful visualization library simga.js, Viewers can easily tell relations between discoveries and whole historical flow.

Vast range

Same as mentioned above. Of course, these contents are based on various references such as papers or well-known publications.
